Once enrolled in a paid plan, the student will receive an email containing information about their assigned counselor. The student can contact their counselor via email, phone, messaging, or a customized dashboard.

If you require assistance, you can reach out to your counselors for a single consultation.

D2Overseas is available to provide guidance if you are uncertain or bewildered about selecting your specialization. We will collaborate with you to determine your passions and skills, so that you can discover the ideal specialization for yourself.

When is the best time to initiate my application process?

It is recommended that students who wish to enroll in an educational institution should begin the application process at least 15 months ahead of the earliest intake period. This will provide them with sufficient time to create an impressive profile and complete the application forms without difficulty.

What all we get to know in 30 minutes free profile consultation call?

During the 30-minute free profile consultation call, you can expect to receive general guidance on your academic background, professional aspirations, and any concerns or questions you may have about the application process. The purpose of this consultation is to determine if our services would be beneficial to you and to assist you in deciding which service would best fit your requirements.

Can I expect my counselor to be available outside of regular business hours and on weekends, considering that I am a working professional?

You may schedule appointments with your counselors on Fridays, depending on their availability. However, if you require a weekend appointment, please inform your counselor in advance so that they can arrange their schedule accordingly.
